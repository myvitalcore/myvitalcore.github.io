Our Origin

Professionals Who Refused to Stay Silent

We met at a medical conference in 2022, comparing notes on a frustrating pattern: patients were following the right advice in fragments, but failing holistically — because no single provider connected all the dots.

A cardiologist's patient was sleeping 4 hours. A dietitian's client was destroying her metabolism with overtraining. The system wasn't designed for coordination — it was designed to keep people coming back.

We still work our day jobs. We still see patients. VitalCore was born in evenings and weekends — because we believed that professional-grade wellness shouldn't cost professional-grade money.
